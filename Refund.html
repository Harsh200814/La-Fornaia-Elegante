Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Order Cancellation

a. Standard Cakes: Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placing the order. After that, cancellation may not be possible.

b. Custom / Theme Cakes: Must be cancelled at least 24 hours before the delivery time. No refund is issued if cancelled later due to preparation work already started.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds are issued only under the following conditions:

a. Wrong Product Delivered
If you receive the wrong item, share a photo within 2 hours. A replacement or refund will be provided.

b. Damaged Product on Delivery
If your order arrives damaged, send clear photos immediately. We will replace it at no extra cost.

c. Quality Issues
If there's a genuine quality issue, contact us within 2 hours. We will review and offer a refund or replacement.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Taste preferences (since flavors vary by individual).
  • Wrong information provided by the customer (address, delivery time, instructions).
  • Custom cake designs with minor variations (as handmade items cannot be 100% identical).
  • Delayed delivery due to weather, traffic, or factors beyond our control.

4. Delivery Issues

If delivery is attempted and the customer is unreachable, the order will be marked as delivered. No refund will be issued.
